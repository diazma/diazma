Dexter the Office Cat

Welcome to the official site of Dexter the office cat

Dexter is an orange tabby cat from Calgary, he is the official office cat of a well-known Oil and Gas company. Dexter was hired to work as a temporary employee after one of the full-time software developers went on a vacation and never came back.

Biography

Dexter was born in the summer of 2013 in the south of Calgary. Dexter's mom was abandoned while heavily pregnant and was rescued by a kind couple that welcome her and the litter into their home. With help from of Meow Foundation, they were able to coordinate the adoption of the kittens and find a forever home for them. Dexter was adopted by a couple in Calgary and relocated to the north-west.

Excited to change the world, Dexter decided to pursue a degree of Computer Science for cats, an experimental degree offered at the University of Victoria (British Columbia). After graduating, he decided to apply for a temporary job as a software developer after a full-time employee disappeared after taking a vacation to an Alaskan cruise. He became the official office cat and it's a successful developer. Dexter likes computers, taking naps, popcorn and drink tea in his tiny teacup.
